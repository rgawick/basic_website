Timberline is the only ski area in North America that is open all 12 months of the year. It has 3,690 vertical feet (more than anybody else in the U.S. Pacific Northwest). It is located near the top of an 11,245-foot volcano (the tallest mountain in Oregon.) Go ahead. Enjoy the stats. Then get up here and enjoy the ride!

Vertical Drop: 3690 ft / 1125 m
Base Elevation: 4850 ft / 1478 m
Summit Elevation: 8540 ft / 2603 m
Skiable Area: 1415 acres
Average Snowfall: 500 in / 1270 cm
